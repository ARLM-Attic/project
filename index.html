FIGHTING
  • Each fight consist of 20 battle turns.
  • Monster health will go down as you fight, even if you don't finish him of within 20 turns.(Considering to add monster health regeneration)
  • If player dies, you will lose 20% of current gold/experience, and will be unable to fight for 5 seconds.
  • If monster dies, player will have a chance to find a drop, it is possible to drop multiple items at once.
  • It is possible that neither a player or a monster dies, it will be called a draw.
  • You wont lose anything but you also won't gain anything when fight is a draw.
ITEMS
  • All monster drop Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic items, higher level monster have more chance to drop each of those items.
  • Last monster on each map is a boss monster, and it has a low chance to drop Legendary item.
  • Boss can also drop any other item and it will be slightly stronger, they will drop more often too.
Potions
  • If you have 30% hp or less potions will be used.
  • Mega potion will be used when your max health is 500 or more.
  • Super potion when its 300 or more.
  • Normal potion when your max health is less than 300.
  • Note that potion is healing you after your battle, not while you fight.
Stats
Each player stat, gives you different bonuses to your secondary stats:
    • Strength:
    • 10 points: 4 Min Damage
    • 10 points: 6 Max Damage
    • 10 points: 3% Critical Damage
    • 10 points: 1 Inventory Slot
    • Endurance:
    • 1 point: 3 Max Health
    • 3 points: 1 Health/second
    • Agility:
    • 100 points: 2% Accuracy
    • 100 points: 3% Evasion
    • 100 points: 3% Critical Chance
    • Dexterity:
    • 2 points: 1 Defense
    • 5 points: 1% Critical damage
    • Wisdom:
    • 2 points: 1 Max Mana
    • 5 points: 1 Mana/seconds
    • Intelligence:
    • 10 points: 1 Max Mana
    • Luck:
    • 100 points: 1% Accuracy
    • 100 points: 2% Evasion
    • 100 points: 1% Critical Chance
    • 10 points: 2% Drop Rate
